## Why CleanShot X is loved by half a million Mac users

- Instant screenshots without shadows & background  
- Scrolling capture (entire webpages, chats)  
- Powerful annotation tools (arrows, text, blur, highlights)  
- Screen recording with mic + clicks  
- Built-in cloud upload & self-destruct links  
- Custom overlays & quick tasks  

This community toolkit gives you **hundreds of ready-to-use overlays, styles, and presets** that take CleanShot to the next level.

## What's inside (instant pro screenshots)

- `Overlays/` — 300+ beautiful backgrounds, devices, mockups  
- `Annotation Styles/` — clean arrows, modern text, step numbers  
- `Themes/` — dark mode, minimal, pastel, retro, neon  
- `Scrolling Templates/` — ready configs for Notion, Figma, websites  
- `Quick Tasks/` — one-click actions (copy, upload, open in editor)  
- `Presets/` — YouTube thumbnails, documentation, bug reports  
- `Icons & Stickers/` — 1000+ elements for annotations

## Installation (macOS) – 60 seconds

1. Click the button → archive downloads  
2. Drag overlays & styles into CleanShot X  
3. Restart CleanShot → everything appears instantly
